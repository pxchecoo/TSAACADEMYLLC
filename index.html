Academic Support

Support that keeps online students focused and moving forward

TSA supports virtual learning with supervision, homework help, personalized tutoring, and a safe environment where students can build routine and confidence.

  • Supervised online learning for students who need structure during virtual school hours.
  • Homework assistance and personalized tutoring for daily academic progress.
  • Safe, structured small group setting with limited spots available.
  • Transportation available as an additional paid service.
Services / Servicios

What TSA provides

Bilingual forms and clear expectations help families stay connected from check-in to check-out.

01

Supervised online learning

Students work in a monitored setting designed for focus, routine, and accountability during virtual learning.

02

Homework assistance

Daily support helps students stay on track with assignments, class requirements, and completion goals.

03

Personalized tutoring
